How I Diagnose Problems

Data points directly to the bottleneck. Here is my diagnostic framework:

    It's a creative or hook issue. Time to test new angles.

  • Good CTR, Low Conversion?

    It's a landing page or offer issue. Optimize CRO and messaging match.

  • High CAC?

    Focus on improving targeting accuracy and BOF retargeting efficiency.
